Our Mission

Goldsmith Chess Club Newsletter (GCCN) was established in July 2018 after two students realized there has never been a chess club for University of London Goldsmith. The main goal of the club is to create a friendly environment for all chess enthusiasts in our university. We require no membership specifically to make chess accessible to even more people. We are always looking forward to welcoming people who are new to chess.
