Join Goldsmith Chess Club Newsletter

(gccn)

Please fill out the following form to formally join our club. GCCN doesn't require any membership fees. If you would like to join our email newsletter, feel free to check the appropriate box in the form. The newsletter emails keep you updated on the latest meetings and tournaments in our club as well as the lastest news in the chess world.



We hope to see you soon in our meetings and tournaments and get to play few rounds of chess with you! If you are new to chess, no worries at all! Our club members are always looking forward to welcoming new members and teaching them the ways of chess! Also, definitely check out our Beginner Lessons, it should be a good starting point!
